## 📋 Task Breakdown by Strategy

### 1. Growth at Fair Price (GAFP)

**Description:** กลยุทธ์ซื้อหุ้นเติบโตสูงในราคาสมเหตุผล

**Research Topics:**
- PEG ratio application in Thai market
- Growth sustainability indicators
- Sector-specific growth rates (Tech, Healthcare)
- Thai growth stock characteristics

**Key Metrics:**
- PEG < 1.5
- Revenue growth > 20%
- EPS growth > 15%
- P/S ratio relative to sector

**Thai Examples:**
- Tech: SYMC, COM7
- Healthcare: BDMS, BH
- Consumer: CPALL, HMPRO

---

### 2. Momentum Investing

**Description:** กลยุทธ์ซื้อหุ้นที่มีแรงซื้อ ราคาวิ่งขึ้น

**Research Topics:**
- Relative strength indicators
- Volume confirmation
- Moving average crossovers
- Thai market momentum patterns

**Key Metrics:**
- Price > SMA50 > SMA200
- RS Rating > 70
- Volume > 1.5x average
- 52-week high proximity

**Thai Considerations:**
- Retail sentiment driven
- Social media impact
- Foreign fund flow tracking

---

### 3. Turnaround/Distressed

**Description:** กลยุทธ์ซื้อหุ้นที่ฟื้นตัวจากปัญหา

**Research Topics:**
- Signs of bottoming
- Management changes
- Restructuring signals
- Debt restructuring in Thailand

**Key Metrics:**
- P/B < 0.5x
- Debt/Equity declining
- Operating margin turning positive
- Cash flow stabilizing

**Thai Examples:**
- Post-COVID recovery stocks
- Restructured companies
- Rights issue recipients

---

### 4. Special Situations

**Description:** กลยุทธ์ลงทุนตามเหตุการณ์พิเศษ

**Research Topics:**
- M&A arbitrage
- Rights issue timing
- Tender offers
- Spin-offs (rare in Thailand)
- Delisting opportunities

**Key Metrics:**
- Spread to offer price
- Deal completion probability
- Timeline to closure
- Regulatory approvals

**Thai Specifics:**
- SET announcements
- SEC filings
- Tender offer rules
- Rights issue mechanics

---

### 5. Small Cap Explorer

**Description:** กลยุทธ์ค้นหา hidden gems ใน small caps

**Research Topics:**
- MAI market characteristics
- Small cap risk factors
- Liquidity considerations
- Information asymmetry

**Key Metrics:**
- Market cap < 5B THB
- Float > 20%
- Daily volume > 1M THB
- NCAV discount

**Thai Considerations:**
- Family ownership concentration
- Related party transactions
- Governance risks
- Limited analyst coverage

---

### 6. Quality Compounder

**Description:** กลยุทธ์ซื้อและถือหุ้นคุณภาพที่ compound ได้

**Research Topics:**
- Moat identification
- Reinvestment rate
- Long-term ROIC
- Management quality

**Key Metrics:**
- ROIC > 15% (5-year average)
- Revenue growth 10-20%
- FCF conversion > 80%
- Debt/Equity < 0.5x

**Thai Examples:**
- CPALL, 7-Eleven franchise
- BDMS, hospital network
- HMPRO, home improvement

---

### 7. Contrarian Investing

**Description:** กลยุทธ์ซื้อตรงข้ามความเชื่อของตลาด

**Research Topics:**
- Sentiment indicators
- Media negativity as signal
- Analyst downgrades
- Insider buying during panic

**Key Metrics:**
- P/E at historical low
- Short interest high
- Negative news flow
- Insider buying > selling

**Thai Application:**
- Crisis opportunities
- Sector rotation extremes
- Political event buying

---

### 8. Sector Rotation

**Description:** กลยุทธ์หมุนเปลี่ยน sector ตาม economic cycle

**Research Topics:**
- Thai economic indicators
- Sector performance by cycle
- Leading/lagging sectors
- Interest rate sensitivity

**Sector Framework:**
| Cycle Phase | Leading Sectors |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Early Recovery | Financials, Consumer |
| Mid Cycle | Technology, Industrials |
| Late Cycle | Energy, Materials |
| Recession | Utilities, Staples |

**Thai Sectors:**
- Banking (KBANK, SCB, BBL)
- Energy (PTT, PTTEP)
- Property (LAND, AP, QH)
- Tourism (AOT, MINT, CENTEL)
