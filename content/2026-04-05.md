


## Alpha Trinity Scanner - Project Summary (2026-04-05)

### Project Overview
**Name:** Alpha Trinity Scanner - Reverse DCF Expectation Gap Analysis
**Market:** Thai Stock Exchange (SET)
**Focus:** Value investing through expectation gap identification

### Core Hypothesis
- Market prices reflect "implied expectations" (growth, margin, ROIC)
- Stocks with expectations EXCEEDING realistic capabilities → Underperform (Growth Traps)
- Stocks with expectations BELOW realistic capabilities → Outperform (Value Opportunities)
