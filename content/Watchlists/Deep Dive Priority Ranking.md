### Skip Criteria
- Low liquidity, financial stocks (harder to analyze)
- P/BV < 1x (mai stock, financial reporting less transparent)
- Lacks tech moat
- Declining profit
- Regulatory issues
- 2-star rating agency (financial sector)
- Limited catalysts
- Commodity exposure
- Turnaround asset
