# Peter Lynch's GARP Framework

> **"Go for a business that any idiot can run – because sooner or later, any idiot probably will."**
> — Peter Lynch, *One Up on Wall Street* (1989)

---

## 🎯 Core Philosophy

### GARP = Growth at Reasonable Price

| Approach | จุดอ่อน | GARP Solution |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| **Pure Value** | Value traps (ถูกเพราะกำลังตาย) | ต้องมี growth ด้วย |
| **Pure Growth** | Nosebleed multiples (แพงเกิน) | ต้องซื้อในราคาสมเหตุผล |
| **GARP** | Balance ทั้งสองอย่าง | P/E ≤ Growth Rate |

### หลักการสำคัญ

```
Earnings Growth = Stock Price Driver (Long-term)

ถ้า earnings เติบโต 10x → stock จะตามไป 10x
คำถามคือ: จ่ายราคาไหนถึง "สมเหตุผล"?
```

### 🔥 Key Quotes

> **"In the long run, it's not just how much you make on your winners, it's how little you lose on your losers."**

> **"Far more money has been lost by investors preparing for corrections, or trying to anticipate corrections, than has been lost in corrections themselves."**

> **"Never invest in any idea you can't illustrate with a crayon."**

> **"Behind every stock is a company. Find out what it's doing."**

## 📊 The PEG Ratio

### Standard Formula

```
PEG Ratio = P/E Ratio / Annual EPS Growth Rate
```

### ⭐ Yield-Adjusted PEG (Lynch's Preferred)

> สำหรับหุ้นที่จ่ายปันผล ต้องปรับเพิ่ม yield เข้าไป

```
Adjusted PEG = P/E Ratio / (EPS Growth Rate + Dividend Yield)
```

**ตัวอย่าง:**
- Company A: P/E 15, Growth 15%, No Dividend → PEG = 15/15 = **1.0**
- Company B: P/E 15, Growth 10%, Dividend 5% → PEG = 15/(10+5) = **1.0**
- → ถ้าไม่ปรับ yield จะคิดว่า B แพงกว่า ทั้งที่จริงๆ เท่ากัน

### PEG Thresholds

| PEG | ความหมาย | Action |
|-----|----------|--------|
| **< 0.5** | Outstanding | ✅ Screaming buy |
| **0.5 - 0.9** | Good | ✅ Buy zone |
| **1.0** | Fair Value | ⚠️ Hold |
| **1.5 - 2.0** | Expensive | ❌ Use caution |
| **> 2.0** | Overvalued | ❌ Danger zone |

### ⚠️ Limitations of PEG

| ปัญหา | คำอธิบาย |
|-------|----------|
| **Growth Projections** | ใช้ estimates ซึ่งมัก optimistic |
| **Cyclicals** | PEG ใช้ไม่ได้กับ cyclical stocks |
| **No Debt Consideration** | ไม่นำหนี้สินมาคิด |
| **One-Year Data** | ต้องใช้ 3-5 year average growth |

## 🏷️ The 6 Stock Categories

> **Critical: อย่าใช้กฎของ category หนึ่งกับอีก category หนึ่ง**

### 1. Slow Growers (Sluggards)

| Characteristic | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| **Growth Rate** | 1-3% ต่อปี |
| **Example** | Utilities, telcos |
| **Why Buy** | Dividend yield สูง ปลอดภัย |
| **Sell Signal** | Dividend cut, yield ต่ำกว่า bond |

### 2. Stalwarts

| Characteristic | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| **Growth Rate** | 10-12% ต่อปี |
| **Example** | KO, PG, JNJ |
| **Why Buy** | Downside protection ใน recession |
| **Lynch's Strategy** | ซื้อตอนถูก ขายตอน +30-50% |
| **Sell Signal** | P/E สูงเกิน historical average |

### 3. Fast Growers ⭐ (The Tenbaggers)

| Characteristic | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| **Growth Rate** | 20-25% ต่อปี |
| **Example** | ร้านค้า expand จาก 10 → 100 สาขา |
| **Why Buy** | ศักยภาพ 10x return |
| **Key Metric** | Same-store sales + expansion room |
| **Sell Signal** | Saturated, P/E > 40, growth slowing |

> 💡 **Lynch's Sweet Spot:** Fast Growers ไม่จำเป็นต้องอยู่ใน industry ที่เติบโตเร็ว แค่มี room to expand

### 4. Cyclicals

| Characteristic | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| **Growth Pattern** | ขึ้นลงตาม economy |
| **Example** | Autos, airlines, steel, chemicals |
| **Counter-Intuitive** | P/E สูงสุด = ช่วง bottom (earnings ต่ำ) |
| **Sell Signal** | กำไรสูงสุด, capacity 100%, สร้างโรงงานใหม่ |

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Cyclicals ดู "ถูก" ที่สุดตอน peak = trap!

### 5. Turnarounds

| Characteristic | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| **Status** | Battered, near bankruptcy |
| **Example** | Bailouts, restructurings, spinoffs |
| **Why Buy** | Uncorrelated with market |
| **Sell Signal** | Turnaround complete → reclassify |

### 6. Asset Plays

| Characteristic | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| **Value Source** | Hidden assets (real estate, patents, cash) |
| **Example** | Land bank > market cap |
| **Why Buy** | Market overlooks asset value |
| **Sell Signal** | Activist/raider unlocks value |

## 🎤 The Two-Minute Drill

> ก่อนซื้อหุ้น ต้องพูดได้ภายใน 2 นาที ให้เด็ก 10 ขวบเข้าใจ

### Structure

```
1. Category: "This is a [Fast Grower/Stalwart/etc]..."
2. Catalyst: "...that just [did something specific]..."
3. Thesis: "They are [expanding/improving/turning around]..."
4. Risks: "The main risk is [specific risk]..."
5. Watch List: "I will watch [specific metric]..."
```

### Example: La Quinta Inns

> "This is a **Fast Grower**. They offer Holiday Inn quality rooms at motel prices by eliminating expensive restaurants. They are successfully replicating across the country. I am watching for debt levels and occupancy rates. **If occupancy drops below 60%, I will re-evaluate.**"

## 📈 Earnings Growth: 5 Drivers

| Driver | How It Works | Category |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| **Reduce costs** | Efficiency gains | Turnarounds |
| **Raise prices** | Pricing power | Stalwarts, Moats |
| **Expand markets** | New geography/demographic | Fast Growers |
| **Sell more** | Same-store growth | All |
| **Fix losers** | Close/sell bad operations | Turnarounds |

### Sustainable Growth Sweet Spot

| Growth Rate | Verdict |
|-------------|---------|
| **< 5%** | Too slow |
| **15-20%** | ✅ Sweet spot |
| **25-30%** | ⚠️ Getting expensive |
| **> 50%** | ❌ Unsustainable |

> 💡 **Lynch Rule:** Avoid hyper-growth (50-100%) มักดึงดูด competition และ sustain ไม่ได้

## 💼 Portfolio Management

### Position Sizing

| Investor Type | Recommended Stocks |
|---------------|-------------------|
| **Individual** | 5-10 stocks (know each well) |
| **Lynch's Fund** | 100-1,400 (planting seeds) |

### The "Flowers vs Weeds" Rule

> **❌ Wrong:** Sell winners at +50% to "lock in gains" while holding losers
> **✅ Right:** Let Fast Growers run as long as story intact, cut losers

```
"If you pull the flowers and water the weeds, you'll end up with a garden full of weeds."
```

### Market Timing Stance

```
"In the long run, a portfolio of well-chosen stocks will always outperform a portfolio of bonds or money-market accounts. In the long run, a portfolio of poorly chosen stocks will always underperform a portfolio of bonds or money-market accounts."
```

> 🔥 **Key:** Lynch stayed **fully invested** - no cash hoarding

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes (The 7 Killers)

| # | Mistake | Why It's Wrong |
|---|---------|----------------|
| 1 | **"It can't go lower"** | Zero is the bottom |
| 2 | **"It can't go higher"** | If earnings double, stock doubles (WMT, MO) |
| 3 | **"Only $3, what can I lose?"** | 100% loss = same as $300 stock |
| 4 | **"They always come back"** | RCA, Blockbuster, Kodak didn't |
| 5 | **"Darkest before dawn"** | Sometimes darkest before pitch black |
| 6 | **Waiting for dividend** | 10% yield + -20% stock = net loss |
| 7 | **Diworsification** | Unrelated M&A destroys value |

## 📋 Practical Screening Checklist

### Core Financial Health

| Metric | Threshold | Why |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| **D/E Ratio** | < 30% | Bankruptcies = debt problem |
| **Net Cash** | Cash/share > Debt/share | Safety buffer |

### GARP Screen

| Metric | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|
| **P/E Ratio** | < 25 |
| **EPS Growth (5Y)** | 15-25% |
| **PEG Ratio** | < 1.0 |

### Quality & Alignment

| Metric | Threshold | Why |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| **Institutional Ownership** | < 60% | Wall Street hasn't found it |
| **Insider Buying** | > 0 | Skin in the game |
| **Share Count** | Decreasing | Buybacks = EPS boost |

## 🏛️ Individual Investor Edge

### Why Individuals Beat Institutions

| Constraint | Institution | Individual |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| **Small-cap access** | ❌ Liquidity rules | ✅ Can buy any size |
| **Benchmark hugging** | ✅ Career risk | ❌ No pressure |
| **Quarterly pressure** | ✅ Must show results | ❌ Long-term focus |
| **Sector limits** | ✅ Mandate restrictions | ❌ Free to roam |
| **"Boring" stocks** | ❌ Can't justify to clients | ✅ Don't care |

### SEC Constraints on Funds

| Rule | Constraint | Impact |
|------|------------|--------|
| **Diversified Fund** | 75% in top 50 positions, max 5% each | Limits concentration |
| **Illiquid Assets** | Max 15% | Blocks small-caps |
| **5/10 Rule** | Max 10% in any one stock | Caps winners |
