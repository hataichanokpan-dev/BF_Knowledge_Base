## Sector Risks

### 1. Global Semiconductor Cycle
**Risk:** Technology spending slowing globally

**Impact:** All electronics manufacturers suffer

**Leading Indicators:**
- Global PMI
- Semiconductor book-to-bill ratio
- Customer inventory levels

### 2. China Slowdown
**Risk:** Major export destination weakening

**Impact:** Reduced demand for Thai electronics

**Monitoring:**
- China PMI
- Trade data
- Currency movements

### 3. Margin Pressure
**Risk:** Competition, rising costs

**Vulnerable:**
- Low value-add manufacturers
- Commodity-type components
