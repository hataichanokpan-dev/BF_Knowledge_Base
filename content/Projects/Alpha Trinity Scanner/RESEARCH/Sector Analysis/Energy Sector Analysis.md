- State-linked (PTT group dominant)

## Sector Risks

### 1. ESG Transition Risk
- Global shift to renewables
- Fossil fuel phasedown
- Stranded asset concerns

### 2. Commodity Volatility
- Oil price sensitivity
- No control over pricing
- Margin compression in downturns

### 3. Regulatory Risk
- Government price controls on fuel
- Tax policy changes
- Environmental regulations

## Investment Implications

### For Value Investors
**Current Opportunity:** ENERGY appears attractive

**Rationale:**
- Sector-wide negative expectation gaps
- Mean reversion potential
- Dividend yield support

**Watchlist Focus:**
