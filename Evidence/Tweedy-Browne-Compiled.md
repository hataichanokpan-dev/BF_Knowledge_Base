## 🔬 Core Finding

### Multiple Value Strategies Work

> **งานวิจัยหลายชิ้นแสดงว่า Value strategies ให้ 15-20%+ annual returns**

Tweedy Browne รวบรวมงานวิจัยหลายฉบับที่ยืนยันว่า:
1. NCAV strategies ให้ผลตอบแทนสูง
2. Low P/E, P/B, P/CF ให้ผลตอบแทนเหนือตลาด
3. ปัจจัยสำคัญ: small caps, low liquidity, longer holding periods

| Graham-Newman | 1930s | ~20% |

---

### 2. Low P/E Strategy

| Study | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **Nicholson (1960)** | Low P/E outperformed 1900-1959 |
| **McWilliams (1966)** | Low P/E beat high P/E by 8% annually |
| **Basu (1977)** | Low P/E earned 16.3% vs 9.3% for high P/E |

---

### 3. Low P/B Strategy

| Study | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **Dreman (1977)** | Low P/B beat market by 6% annually |
| **Rosenberg (1985)** | B/P effect confirmed in 1978-1984 |
| **Fama-French (1992)** | HML factor = 4-6% annually |

---

### 4. Low P/CF Strategy

| Study | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **Haugen (1995)** | Low P/CF beat market significantly |
| **LSV (1994)** | Low P/CF = 19.1% annually |
