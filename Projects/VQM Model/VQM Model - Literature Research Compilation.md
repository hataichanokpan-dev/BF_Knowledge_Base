## Part 1: Core Foundation Papers

### Value Investing Literature

| Paper | Year | Journal | Key Finding | Relevance |
|-------|------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Fama & French | 1992 | Journal of Finance | Cross-section of stock returns explained by size & book-to-market | Core Value factor definition |
| Fama & French | 1993 | Journal of Financial Economics | FF 3-factor model (Market, Size, Value) | Foundation for multi-factor models |
| Fama & French | 2015 | Journal of Financial Economics | FF 5-factor model (added Profitability, Investment) | Quality factor inclusion |
| Lakonishok et al. | 1994 | Journal of Finance | Contrarian strategies work (value stocks outperform) | Value premium evidence |
| Graham & Dodd | 1934 | Book | Security Analysis | Value investing philosophy |

**Summary:** Value premium is well-documented across markets. Low P/B, P/E stocks tend to outperform. FF 5-factor explicitly includes profitability (Quality) and investment (anti-growth) factors.

### Momentum Literature

| Paper | Year | Journal | Key Finding | Relevance |
|-------|------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Jegadeesh & Titman | 1993 | Journal of Finance | 3-12 month momentum persists | Core momentum definition |
| Jegadeesh & Titman | 2001 | Journal of Finance | Momentum holds beyond 1990s | Robustness check |
| Rouwenhorst | 1998 | Journal of Finance | Momentum in emerging markets | EM applicability |
| Asness | 1994 | University of Chicago | Momentum and value coexist | Factor interaction |

**Summary:** Momentum is one of the most robust anomalies. 6-12 month price continuation is documented globally, including emerging markets.

### Quality Factor Literature

| Paper | Year | Journal | Key Finding | Relevance |
|-------|------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Novy-Marx | 2013 | Journal of Financial Economics | Gross profitability predicts returns | Quality proxy |
| Sloan | 1996 | Accounting Review | Accruals negatively correlated with returns | Earnings quality |
| Piotroski | 2000 | Journal of Accounting Research | F-score improves value selection | Composite quality metric |
| Asness et al. | 2019 | Journal of Portfolio Management | Quality Minus Junk factor | Quality definition |

**Summary:** Quality (profitability, earnings quality, low leverage) is a persistent return predictor. ROIC and FCF conversion are key metrics.

### Multi-Factor Models

| Paper | Year | Journal | Key Finding | Relevance |
|-------|------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Asness et al. | 2013 | Journal of Portfolio Management | Value and Momentum Everywhere | Combined factors work |
| Blitz et al. | 2014 | Journal of Asset Management | Residual momentum vs standard | Momentum refinement |
